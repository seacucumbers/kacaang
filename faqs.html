Cashew nuts are a nutrient-rich snack that can contribute to overall health. Packed with protein, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals, they support heart health by improving cholesterol levels and may aid in weight management due to their satiating properties.
With a lower glycemic index, cashews can help regulate blood sugar levels. Additionally, their antioxidant content promotes skin health, and essential minerals like magnesium and phosphorus contribute to bone health.
As a plant-based protein source, cashews are particularly beneficial for vegetarians and vegans. While enjoying the benefits, it's crucial to consume them in moderation due to their calorie density.
