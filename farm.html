How we achieve the Perfect Cashews
Step 1
Hand pick the best quality cashews from our farm.
Step 2
Dry cashews in the sunlight for a few days before roasting.
Step 3
Remove the outer and inner shell by cutting and peeling.
Step 4
Cashews are tossed and coated with our homemade flavourings.
